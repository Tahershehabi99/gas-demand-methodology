About this site

For every country in the EU plus the UK, we calculate monthly gas demand broken down into three sectors:

  • Power — gas burned in power stations to generate electricity
  • Industry — gas used by factories, refineries, chemicals, steel, etc.
  • Homes & Businesses — gas used for heating homes, offices, shops, restaurants, etc.

The methodology differs by country because each country publishes different data at different speeds. Pick a country above to see exactly where each number comes from.
