About this site

For every country in the EU plus the UK, we calculate monthly gas demand broken down into three sectors:

  • Power — gas burned in power stations to generate electricity
  • Industry — gas used by factories, refineries, chemicals, steel, etc.
  • Homes & Businesses — gas used for heating homes, offices, shops, restaurants, etc.

The methodology differs by country because each country publishes different data at different speeds. The key reference point in every country is Eurostat (or DESNZ for the UK) — the official statistical agency that publishes monthly gas balances about 2 months after each month ends. So for any given month there are typically two states: before Eurostat reports (preliminary) and after Eurostat reports (final). Pick a country above to see the details.
